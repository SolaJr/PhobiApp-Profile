How it works


PhobiApp uses classical conditioning and virtual reality to simulate and train a user to alter their reactions to public speaking. Their are 3 main parts to this system: the phone, the heart reader, and the vibrator .

1) Download the App
‍
To use this app, it is recomeded you use a VR headset holder to operate it, but you can also just hold the phone up to your eye level to get similar results.

2) Select an enviorment

3) Connect your Vibrator and Fitbit ( if applicable) 

4) Give your speech

5) View your suggestions

6) Plan your next practice session
‍
When you start the app, press start mode. As you give your speech in an environment of your choice, you get to view live data about your speech such as how many filler words you are using or how fast your heart rate is going. These general factors are used to calculate how good or bad a speech may be based on customizable criteria. Once you start showing bad habits, your vibrator will vibrate very harshly and when you are doing well, it will vibrate very softly. This information will tell you general things about your speech that can be easily corrected the more times you give your speech.

After enough practice sessions and using the feedback with the vibrator, your body will become classically conditioned to showing the good public speaking habits the app showed you while you felt the light vibration instead of the bad habits you felt during the harsh virbration.
